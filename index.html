Contact

My musical inspiration comes from the 'standards', those timeless popular tunes from the great songwriters of the golden age of Swing and Jazz.

In addition to my favourite 50 classic standards, I sing from a catalogue of newer songs that, I think you'll agree, hit the mark. So if you love music that's rich and exciting, with lyrics that youch your heart, then you and I are friends already.

I perform live, accompanied by great musicians, and it's my pleasure to put on a great show no matter how big or small your event. I look forward to hearing from you.

I only work with live (often lively) musicians so if I can help you with music for your event or project please email or message me.
