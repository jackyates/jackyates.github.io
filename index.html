Hi, my name is Jack and I recently discovered that I'm a singer.

'Better late than never' or so the saying goes, I find myself starting out on an adventure that I won't put any limits on. From where I am now, there's not much to say about my musical achievements, but I'm hoping that before too long there'll be lots of stories to tell. For now I just need to carry on writing, arranging, singing and recording the music I love.

My musical inspiration comes from the 'standards', those timeless popular tunes from the great songwriters. So if like me, you love music that's rich and layered and beautiful and exciting, and you've just got to have lyrics that touch your heart then I guess you and I are friends already.

I only work with live (often lively) musicians so if I can help you with music for your event or project please email or message me.
